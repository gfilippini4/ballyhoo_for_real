The Aloha State is getting their very own Chick-fil-A.

America’s favorite fast food chain made the announcement that is was opening a location on the island of Oahu.

"Like all Chick-fil-A restaurants, this location will be owned and operated by a local resident and employ approximately 80 team members," the company said, in a news release.

Beyond the island, there is no official word on where the chicken restaurant will be located exactly – or when it will open.
